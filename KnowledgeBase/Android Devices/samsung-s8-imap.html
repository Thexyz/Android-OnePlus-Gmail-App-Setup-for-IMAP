This is a setup guide that will assist you in setting up your Hosted Mailbox with the native mail app on Samsung mobile and tablet devices, this is the default email app that comes pre installed on Samsung devices, so there is no need to download any app from Google Play store.  You can setup your devices using IMAP so you can send email, attach files etc from your device and have it fully sync with the server.  IMAP is the preferred protocol for setting up email on Samsung Galaxy devices, this way if you permanently delete the account, all mail data will be restored once setup again. If you have upgraded to the MobileSync service (Exchange ActiveSync), you can see how to setup with Samsung Galaxy with MobileSync. If you prefer to to have app access through a Gmail account, you can follow this guide for setting up a Google account with IMAP. 

    1. Locate the "Settings" icon within your Apps Drawer on your Samsung phone or device.

    1. Within Settings, scroll down and select "Add Account".

    1. Select "Email".

    1. Enter the following information:
      1. "Email Address": Your email address
      2. "Password": Your email address password

    1. Tap "Next".
    2. Select "IMAP account" within the Account Setup screen as the type of email to setup.

    1. Enter the following incoming email server information:
      1. "Username": Your email address
      2. "Password": Your email address password
      3. "IMAP Server": secure.emailsrvr.com
      4. "Security type": SSL
      5. "Port": 993
      6. "IMAP path prefix": Leave this field blank

    1. Tap "Next".
    2. Enter the following information:
      1. "SMTP server": secure.emailsrvr.com
      2. "Security type": SSL
      3. "Port": 465
      4. "Require sign-in": Checked
      5. "User name": Your email address
      6. "Password": Your email address password

    1. Tap "Next".
    2. Adjust the displayed mailbox settings, if desired.

    1. Tap "Next".
    2. Within the "Set up email" screen, enter your Email Address and your Name.

  1. Tap "Done".
  2. Success! Your Samsung device should now begin syncing with your mailbox.

If you need to setup your email account on other devices, you can find our email help setup tool on the support page.
